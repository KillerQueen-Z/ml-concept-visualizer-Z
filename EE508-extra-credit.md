# EE508 Extra Credit for Repo Contributions

EE508 students can earn up to **5% extra credit on the final course grade** by submitting a strong pull request to this repo.

Repo: https://github.com/ourarash/ml-concept-visualizer

## Eligible contributions

Your PR should do one of these:

- Add a new interactive, animated visualization for an ML, deep learning, NLP, systems, or hardware concept relevant to the course
- Make a substantial improvement to an existing visualization, such as better pedagogy, interactivity, animation, accuracy, usability, or visual design

## Requirements

- The work must be your own. Vibe coding is ok.
- The work should not duplicate an existing visualization or overlap heavily with an open PR
- The submission must be an interactive visualization with animation, not a mostly static page
- The visualization should clearly help students understand the concept better
- The visualization must be technically correct and clearly explain the concept
- The page must run cleanly in the browser and be usable on desktop and mobile
- The PR must include a short description, screenshots or a short video, and a note on what changed educationally
- You must respond to review feedback before the deadline

## How to submit

1. Fork the repo: https://github.com/ourarash/ml-concept-visualizer
2. Create a branch for your change
3. Add your new visualization or improve an existing one
4. Open a pull request against the main repo
5. In the PR description, include:

- Your name and email address
- What concept you covered or improved
- Why the change helps students learn
- Screenshots or a short video
- Anything specific you want reviewed

## Rubric

Up to **5% total**:

- **1%**: Concept choice is relevant and meaningful for EE508
- **1%**: Technical content is correct and educationally clear
- **1%**: Interaction and visualization design genuinely help understanding
- **1%**: Implementation quality is solid and polished
- **1%**: PR is complete, well documented, and merge-ready

Small or mostly cosmetic changes may receive partial credit or no credit.
